About the project

This is rather a fun fact - this was one of the first "project" I have ever made. Unfortunately I lost the source code to that mod. Here is a quick summary of it:

This mod explores an alternate history where Polish politician Ryszard Petru (leader of the modern-era party Nowoczesna) travels back in time to 1936, becomes President of Poland, and steers the nation toward a progressive, reformist path. The mod introduces a unique narrative blending humor, political satire, and strategic gameplay, allowing players to reshape interwar Poland into a liberal democracy or a technocratic powerhouse.
Key Features:

  • Focus tree: "Petru’s Modernization": Focuses on economic liberalization, educational reforms, and technological advancements.
    Satirical tree: "Time Traveler’s Gambit": Unlocks futuristic technologies (e.g., early computers) and absurd events (e.g., Petru debating historical figures). "Democracy First": Strengthens democratic institutions, aligns with Western allies, and avoids authoritarianism.
  • Decisions & Events::
    Economic Reforms: Implement free-market policies, privatize state industries, and attract foreign investment.
    Political Events: Satirical events where Petru clashes with historical figures like Piłsudski or negotiates with Hitler using modern economic jargon.
  • Unique Mechanics:
    "Time Paradox": A stability penalty if Petru’s actions disrupt historical timelines too severely.
  • National Spirits:
    "Petru's Vision": Grants research bonuses and consumer goods factory efficiency.
